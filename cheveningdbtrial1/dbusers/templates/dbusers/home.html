About Us

The Chevening scholarship alumni association is a registered community-based group in Nairobi, Kenya. Its members consist of former scholars funded by the Chevening scholarship program by the British Foreign, Commonwealth, and Development Office (FCDO) to pursue their Master's studies in the UK. The association aims to harness the collective synergies, experience, and expertise of these young professionals for the development of Kenya.
